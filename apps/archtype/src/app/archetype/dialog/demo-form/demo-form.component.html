Overview

Deskera is a leading cloud-based business software provider in South East Asia. Deskera has been recognized as the #1 Integrated Business Applications as a Service (BAaaS) provider in 2015 for the South East Asian market by Frost & Sullivan. Today, our cloud services power more than 3,000 SMEs and businesses globally. We are headquartered in Singapore and have offices in India, Malaysia and Indonesia along with presence in the rest of South East Asia. Deskera products serve a wide range of industries such as manufacturing, trading, distribution, engineering, building and construction. For over a decade, we have proved our mettle as a company that is focused on creating simple and robust enterprise software that drives progress for our clients.

The Future Looks Promising

The present market displays an exponential growth in the demand for cloud solutions that drive business operations with enormous amount of data being processed everyday. Deskera products with its Big Data backbone offer a much required industry tool for devising new solutions that cater to a large customer base. Our focus is to configure our products to fit customer needs and industries of all sizes. Within a short span of about 8 years, we have become a leading provider of cloud services that facilitates process automation. We aim to keep up the momentum of making businesses simpler, user-friendly and automated. With a customer-centric approach, our aim is to help enterprises and businesses thrive.
